Real Estate Staging Intensive

A 3 day Real Estate Staging Training
(home staging training) Workshop

Looking to start a business in home staging? Then this Certificate program in real estate staging (also called home staging), which has been approved by the Commissioner of Higher Education for the State of Connecticut, is just what you need!

During this 3 day real estate staging training (home staging training) program you will learn how to help your clients prepare their homes to sell quickly and for top dollar.

To do this, we will discuss the following:

  • The philosophy of real estate staging
  • What is truly important in preparing a home for sale
  • Specific information on how to differentiate this home from others on the market both inside and out
  • Vacant home staging
  • Decorating for the "model home" look

In addition to the specific information on preparing a home for sale, we will discuss the business aspect of real estate staging, including:

  • How to attract customers
  • Maintaining client relationships
  • Specific marketing tools to use to open doors
  • Steps to take to begin your business and move it forward in the coming years.

But we’re not done yet...as part of our home staging training, we will have the opportunity to use our new found skills as we use the principles of real estate staging to prepare a home for sale!

That’s not all! At the end of your real estate staging training course, you will receive:

  • A Certificate of Completion in Real Estate Staging
  • On-going support whenever you would like, as you embark on your exciting new career.
  • Membership eligibility for the Association of Design Education. The ADE will continue to provide you the on-going educational support you will need as you start and grow your business.
