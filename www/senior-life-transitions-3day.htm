3-Day Senior Life Transition
Professional Workshop

...an intensive 3-day workshop that will prepare you to assist seniors with the move to the next phase of their lives - including how to start and market your business!

 

Our 3-Day Senior Life Transition Professional Workshop is designed to teach you every aspect of working with the elderly as they transition as well as how to start and market your business - and one that will only become more and more important as Baby Boomers move into their retirement years and beyond.  Our Certificate Program has been approved by the Commissioner of Higher Education for the State of Connecticut, which ensures your training meets the high standards set for private occupational schools.

 

Through reading, discussion, videos, and role plays, this workshop provides you with the information you need to learn how to assist your senior clients and their families during this important phase of their lives.

 

During this workshop you will learn:

  • How to understand the senior client, enabling you to approach, communicate, and work with them comfortably.
  • How to help your senior client transition from their current home into smaller quarters, assisted living, or long-term care - or to age in place.
  • Necessary safety precautions to consider when working with seniors, including space/floor planning, lighting, moving considerations - and so much more!
  • How to add this special service for seniors to your current business, or start a new business.
  • The key steps in marketing this new service.

But that's not all.  At the end of the training, you will receive a Certificate of Completion as a Senior Life Transition Professional, as well as one-year free membership in the National Association of Senior Move Managers, the only professional association in the country dedicated to helping the rapidly increasing 55+ population with middle and later life transition issues.  You will continue to receive on-going support from the School of Interior Redesign whenever you would like as you embark on your exciting new career.  You are also able to join the Association of Design Education, which will continue to provide you the on-going educational support you will need as you start and grow your business.

To Enroll in the Three-Day Senior life Transition Professional Workshop:

 

Cost for this 3-day Senior Life Transition Professional Workshop held in Milford, Connecticut is $1,995, which includes all of the above, as well as lunches, beverages and snacks each day.  A $500 deposit is required to reserve your space in the program.  The balance is due two weeks before class begins. 

 

[If you are interested in having senior life transition training in your area, the cost is $2,750 per student, with a minimum of four students. For additional information - or to make arrangements for another area - contact the School of Interior Redesign.]

To see the schedule of our Senior Life Transition workshops, check the Calendar for more information. 

 

To reserve your space in a 3-day Senior Life Transition workshop offered by the School of Interior Redesign, complete the Application and submit, along with your deposit of $500.  The balance is due two weeks prior to the start of class.

Cancellation Policy:  A full refund will be given if the student cancels ten days prior to the first day of class.  A 75% refund will apply if the student cancels at anytime after the ten day cancellation period.  No refund will be given after the start of classes.  However, should a student need to cancel or leave due to an emergency, the tuition will be applied to another scheduled class.  Refunds will be mailed to the student within fourteen days of cancellation.
