Interior Redesign Intensive

A 3-day Interior Redesign Workshop

If you already have a business, but are interested in adding to it by offering interior redesign services, our three-day Interior Redesign Intensive Workshop is just for you.

During this workshop, you'll learn:

  • An extremely detailed analysis of interior design (one day decorating), including the principles and techniques critical to your success as an Interior Redesign Specialist.
  • Thte step-by-step process necessary for a successful redesign.
  • The design rules used by all successful interior designers and decorators to insure a beautiful, comfortable living space.
  • Basic interior decorating guidelines to enhance your knowledge base.

We Provide Hands-On Interior Redesign Training

In addition to covering this material during class, you will also complete two hands-on interior redesigns, which will reinforce the information learned during classroom time. When class is over, training materials you will receive will continue to be an excellent resource as you grow your business.

To Enroll in the Interior Redesign Intensive

Cost for the three days of interior redesign training is $1,500, which includes all of the above, as well as lunch, beverages, and snacks each day.

To see the schedule of interior redesign classes, check our Class Schedule or contact the School of Interior Redesign LLC for more information.

To reserve your space in any interior redesign class, complete the Application and submit it, along with an initial deposit of $500. The balance is due two weeks prior to the start of class.

Cancellation Policy: A full refund will be given if the student cancels ten days prior to the first day of class. A 75% refund will apply if the student cancels at anytime after the ten day cancellation period. No refund will be given after the start of classes. However, should a student need to cancel or leave due to an emergency, the tuition will be applied to another scheduled class. Refunds will be mailed to the student within fourteen days of cancellation.
