Marketing Your Design-Related Business

A half-day workshop

Ready to kick off your business but not quite sure how to do that? Do you have all of the design ability you need to have a business in interior decorating, interior redesign, or real estate staging but just don't know how to get customers? Have you started your design-related business, but are wondering where the clients are and how you can reach them?

Marketing Your Design-Related Business is just the tool you need! Written from a designer's perspective, this workshop is jam-packed with creative ideas, and instruction on how you can implement these ideas for very little money to effectively market your business.

During this course, you will learn the secrets of locating your potential clients, and getting them to buy your services!

Some of the things you will learn include:

  • Understanding the marketing umbrella: advertising, marketing, promotion, public relations and publicity, and sales
  • Knowing your market
  • Branding your business: Creating the "look" and "feel" of your business
  • How to communicate effectively with your client, both verbally and in writing
  • The tools necessary to market in print, through the internet, and face to face
  • Public speaking and presentation techniques and ideas
  • How to create a marketing plan and measure your results
  • Resources

...As well as a myriad of business forms that will keep you and your business ready for success.

With the step-by-step process provided, you will know exactly what needs to be done in order to market your design-related business using tried and true techniques that keep you one step ahead of the competition.
