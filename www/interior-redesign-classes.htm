Interior Redesign Classes

Interior redesign, or using the homeowner's own things to create warm, inviting spaces, is becoming more and more popular, as people realize the incredible difference this practical, less expensive alternative to traditional interior decorating or design can make in their homes. And now, through the School of Interior Redesign LLC, you can learn the art of interior redesign (sometimes called one day decorating), whether to start a new career, add to your existing business, or to simply use in your own home.

Introduction to Interior Redesign

Through our one-day seminar in interior redesign, you will learn how to have the home of your dreams - without buying a thing! This seminar also provides an excellent introduction to interior redesign for those interested in the possibility of a new career.

Interior Redesign Intensive

Our three-day interior redesign workshop provides all the necessary training to learn the art of redesign. Included in this three-day Intensive are classroom time and two hands-on interior redesigns to provide the opportunity to practice what you've learned.

Interior Redesign/Real Estate Staging Intensive

If you would like to learn both interior redesign for those clients staying in the home and real estate staging for your selling clients, this four-day comprehensive workshop is for you! Complete with classroom time, two hands-on redesigns, and one hands-on staging, you’ll be ready to incorporate redesign and staging into your current business. (If you are interested in starting a redesign and staging business, please see The Business of Design)

Interior Redesign Home Study

Our interior redesign home study, teaches you the art of interior redesign, at your own pace and working in the comfort of your own home. Broken into modules for ease of learning, the comprehensive manual and accompanying workbook provide all of the information and exercises needed to ensure your complete and thorough understanding of interior redesign.

Interior Redesign and Real Estate Staging Home Study

If your goal is to learn both interior redesign and real estate staging, the interior redesign and real estate staging home study is what you’re looking for! This home study program is filled with everything you need to know about interior redesign and real estate staging. Included are a comprehensive manual and accompanying workbook filled with information and exercises to ensure your complete understanding of redesign and staging.

So check out the interior redesign workshops now for further information...and begin living your dream!
