Real Estate Staging Intensive

A 2-day real estate staging training (home staging training)

Are you interested in learning all there is to know about Real Estate Staging?  Then this real estate staging training workshop is perfect for you!:

During this workshop you will learn:
  • The philosophy of real estate staging
  • What are the areas to spend the most money–and which aren't
  • What are the home 'hot spots' that will make—or break—the deal.
  • Specific information on how to differentiate this home from others on the market, both inside and out.
  • Basic information on vacant home staging
  • Decorating for the ‘model home’ look

We Provide Hands-On Real Estate Staging Training

In addition to covering this material during class, you will also complete a hands-on staging of a house ready to go on the market, which will reinforce the information learned during classroom time.

To Enroll in the Real Estate Staging Intensive:

Cost for the 2 day Real Estate Staging Training (home staging training) for the Professional training workshop is $1,000 which includes all of the above, as well as lunch, and beverages and snacks each day.

To see the schedule of real estate staging training (home staging training) classes, check the Class Schedule  for more information.

To reserve your space in any interior redesign training or real estate staging class, complete the Application and submit it, along with your deposit of $500. The balance is due two weeks prior to the start of class. One day classes must be paid in full at the time of enrollment.

Cancellation Policy: A full refund will be given if the student cancels ten days prior to the first day of class. A 75% refund will apply if the student cancels at anytime after the ten day cancellation period. No refund will be given after the start of classes. However, should a student need to cancel or leave due to an emergency, the tuition will be applied to another scheduled class. Refunds will be mailed to the student within fourteen days of cancellation.
